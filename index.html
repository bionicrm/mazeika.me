Hey,

My name is T.J. Mazeika. Outside of school, I work as a student sound engineer and I.T. technician. At home, I develop web and system applications. Thus far, I am proficient in Java, Go, Dart, PHP and other web languages, and am acquainted with Scala, C/C++, Python, Ruby, Rust, Kotlin, Groovy, and a few others. I also mine cryptocurrencies, specifically Ethereum and Verium, using 3 RX580's and dual Xeon X5690's.

Among other things, I have an affinity for high-end computing hardware and emerging technologies that embrace the connected world, most notably the blockchain, but also smaller-scale networks like Internet of Things devices. I like building things, and even coming up with new ideas, either for the betterment of the world or for sheer pleasure. Now that I'm applying for college, I aim to study computer science for a deeper understanding of the subject and for the love of learning; I have big plans for the future, and it's only a matter of time.
